Welcome to Little Flower Preschool

Where every child blossoms! Located in the heart of Silvassa, we are guided by four passionate educators with decades of experience.

About Us

Little Flower Preschool is committed to nurturing young minds in Silvassa with a focus on playful learning, safety, and community values. Our curriculum is based on the CBSE pattern, ensuring a strong academic foundation along with holistic development.

Our Programmes

  • Playgroup
  • Nursery
  • Kindergarten (KG)
  • Special Activities: Art, Music, Outdoor Play

Curriculum: Based on CBSE pattern tailored for preschoolers.

Unique Features

  • Founded and run by four talented teachers: Sonali Deshmukh, Khushboo Hisaria, Deepika Rathore, and Devika Das
  • Each founder brings years of experience in teaching and running preschools in Silvassa
  • Strong focus on emotional and social development
  • Safe and secure campus with CCTV and dedicated childcare staff
  • Community involvement and local festival celebrations
